Janet and George want to welcome you to Grey Gables Farmhouse B&B. Grey Gables was established over 50 years ago by Janet's parents. They bought the old farmhouse and renovated it, built onto it to become a place to welcome tourists from all over the world. We bought it in 2017 and continue to welcome folk. We aim to give you a warm welcome, comfort and good food and to leave refreshed and ready to explore more of our beautiful country.

Situated just off the N71 to West Cork, very close to Innishannon Village, Kinsale (the Gourmet capital of Ireland!!) and Cork City also the gateway to West Cork and the Wild Atlantic Way. It is a working Farm. There are 30 acres (11 hectares). We raise Maiden Stock for our son. The Cattle range in age from Calf to 2 year olds. We have 2 dogs - Molly the Collie and Pip the Jack Russel plus Ty the cat!! Janet loves her garden and you are welcome to wander around and find a quiet spot to relax and enjoy the sun set and listen to the birdsong.

Attractions in the Area

From Grey Gables Farmhouse it is so easy to reach Cork City with all its tourist attractions. You have Blarney Castle and beautiful gardens. Cork City Goal steeped in history. Shandon (St Marys ) Cathedral, The English Market with its great food, St Finbarrs Cathedral and many more, also there are boat rides around the harbour and bus tours to various locations.

Kinsale

Kinsale is 12 km with all its beauty, situated by the sea, the sound of the boats clinking in the harbour and such a variety of places to eat, too many to mention so book a place for the evening!

Cobh

Cobh is 40km, a great place for a day out. Spike Island is a must, Cobh Cathedral, The Titanic Experience and the Heritage centre will keep you busy for the day.

Wild Atlantic Way

When leaving Grey Gables and travelling towards Killarney you can travel the wild atlantic way with it rugged beauty and breathtaking view of the Sea. Such beauty around every corner , a not to be missed experience.

So get packing and make your reservation at Grey Gables and I promise you won't be disappointed.
